Phase 2 pivotal study design

The largest prospective study in relapsed or refractory (R/R) all to date{{citationNumber[7]}}

Inclusion Criteria{{citationNumber[6]}}:
● Age ≥ 18 years
● Ph-negative R/R B-precursor ALL
ο Early relapse (duration of first remission ≤ 12 months)
OR
ο Any relapse or refractory disease after first salvage therapy
OR
ο Relapse within 12 months of allogeneic HSCT
AND
ο ≥ 10% blasts in bone marrow

Exclusion Criteria{{citationNumber[6]}}:
● Late relapse patients (a CR lasting for > 12 months)

Phase 2, open-label, multicenter, single-arm clinical trial{{citationNumber[6]}}

Study Endpoints{{citationNumber[6]}}-{{citationNumber[9]}}
Primary
● CR/CRh* within the first 2 cycles of treatment
Secondary
● CR
● CRh*
● Duration of response (DOR)/relapse-free survival (RFS)
● HSCT realization
● Incidence of adverse reactions
Exploratory
● Minimal residual disease (MRD) response was defined as MRD by polymerase chain reaction (PCR) < 1 x 10-4
Endpoint assessment was determined by bone marrow biopsies/MRD testing (every 28 days).
Phase 2 pivotal study results

Primary endpoint (CR/CRh*) achieved
During the first 2 treatment cycles of BLINCYTO®

● Probability of attaining CR/CRh* was not affected by age, history of transplant, number of prior salvage therapies, or primary refractory status{{citationNumber[7]}}
● 81% of responses occurred within Cycle 1 (n=62/77){{citationNumber[6]}}

Secondary and exploratory endpoints achieved
